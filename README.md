# Stratton Heights Store Data Analysis  
## Interactive Sales Dashboard using Microsoft Excel

---

## Project Overview  
This project focuses on building an interactive sales dashboard using the 2025 sales dataset to analyze business performance and uncover actionable insights.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly understand revenue trends, customer behavior, and key business drivers.

---


## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Executive Summary Metrics
- Total Revenue: $1,266,390  
- Total Profit: $634,256  
- Total Orders: 8,000  
- Average Order Value (AOV): $158  
- Profit Margin: 50%  

---

## Business Questions Answered

1. Sales Performance Over Time  
   - Monthly and quarterly sales trends  

2. Top Products and Categories  
   - Revenue contribution by category and product  

3. Customer Segmentation  
   - Identification of high-value customers  

4. Geographic Analysis  
   - Regional sales distribution  

5. Sales Channel Performance  
   - Website vs Mobile vs In-Store comparison  

---

## Process

- Data Cleaning  
  - Checked for missing values and inconsistencies  
  - Standardized data types and formats  

- Data Analysis  
  - Created Pivot Tables for each business question  
  - Aggregated and structured data for insights  

- Dashboard Creation  
  - Combined all Pivot Tables into a single dashboard  
  - Added slicers for interactivity and filtering

## Key Insights

### Sales Trend
- Sales showed a cyclical pattern  
- Strong growth in Q4, peaking in November and December  
- First half averaged approximately $85,000 per month  



### Product Performance
- Tech and Electronics generated approximately 40% of total revenue  
- Smart Fitness Watch was the top-performing product  


### Customer Insights
- Steve Jones is our overall top performing customer by revenue

### Geographic Performance
- Western Region leads with 38% of total sales  


### Sales Channels
- Direct Website is the top-performing channel  

## Final Conclusion

The business closed 2025 in a strong financial position, maintaining a consistent 50% profit margin.


## Strategic Recommendation

Focus on Website Growth Strategy

Since the Direct Website is the most profitable channel:
- Invest in website optimization  
- Improve user experience  
- Scale digital marketing effort
